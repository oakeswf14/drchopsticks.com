The Legend with Two Sticks

The Quiet Life of a Not‑So‑Silent Assassin

A Ninja’s Appetite for Adventure

By day, Dr. Chopsticks is less rooftop-stalking assassin and more connoisseur of extra-cheesy baked ziti—his stealth routinely compromised by the rustle of takeout bags and the glow of late-night delivery apps.

His black gi tells stories of battles mostly fought from the couch: anime marathons that stretch until sunrise, and Pokémon debates over which team could actually take down a legendary. Plans for silent rooftop ambushes are drawn in meticulous detail, then postponed in favor of “just one more episode.”

Yet beneath the sauce stains and stacked manga volumes beats the heart of a true warrior—loyal, oddly disciplined in his own way, and always ready to trade a night of crime-fighting for good food, great stories, and the comforting clack of chopsticks.
