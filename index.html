The Legend with Two Sticks

The Quiet Life of a Not‑So‑Silent Assassin

A Ninja’s Appetite for Adventure.
By day, Dr. Chopsticks is less shadowy assassin and more connoisseur of extra-cheesy baked ziti—his stealth routinely compromised by the rustle of takeout bags. His black gi fits a little snugger with every side quest, but he insists it’s “battle-ready” as long as he can sit comfortably through an anime marathon. When he isn’t plotting rooftop ambushes he never quite attempts, he’s ranking his favorite Pokémon and arguing which one could actually solo a legendary. Yet beneath the sauce stains and towering stacks of manga beats the heart of a true warrior: loyal, determined, and always ready to trade a night of crime-fighting for one more episode
