Of The Dr. Chopsticks Saga

The Heroes, The Frienemies & The One Pulling The Strings

Dr. Chopsticks
Ziti Samurai • Energy Chopstick Wielder • Midnight Binger

Part urban legend, part walking food review, Dr. Chopsticks is a ninja whose greatest quests almost always end at a buffet line. He wields twin Energy Chopsticks—ancient weapons forged to defeat evil, now repurposed to test the structural integrity of baked ziti and deliver molten cheese with surgical precision.

When he’s not defending alleyways and late‑night diners from culinary injustice, he’s binge‑watching anime, ranking Pokémon teams, and meticulously documenting every plate of pasta he encounters. Equal parts warrior and dork, he is driven by a strange but noble credo: no villain, and no under‑seasoned sauce, shall go unchallenged.

The Lanky Leaf
7‑Foot Ninja • Emerald Acrobat • Ride‑Or‑Die Best Friend

Towering at a ridiculous seven feet tall, The Lanky Leaf moves like a bamboo stalk in a hurricane—flexible, unpredictable, and somehow always in frame. Clad in green and yellow, he began as Dr. Chopsticks’ rival, clashing in a legendary battle that destroyed half a buffet and ended in a truce over anime spoilers and shared carbs.

Since high school, the two have been partners in both real crime‑ fighting and its far more important cousin: culinary crime‑fighting. United by an unapologetic love of anime, Japanese culture, and over‑complicated battle strategies, The Lanky Leaf uses his absurd height for everything from rooftop reconnaissance to grabbing snacks from the top shelf without breaking stride.

The Matzo Mercenary
Frenemy For Life • Debate Tank • Carb‑Powered Enigma

The Matzo Mercenary has been both friend and adversary to Dr. Chopsticks for as long as anyone can remember. Their bond is built on an unshakable foundation of shared passions—sports, comfort food, and extremely specific opinions about both—that they somehow never agree on at the same time.

Proudly Jewish and perpetually argumentative, he doesn’t just enter debates; he weaponizes them. He’ll take the opposite side of any topic on principle, just to see if Dr. Chopsticks can keep up. These long‑running arguments over teams, toppings, and “what actually counts as canon” have sparked rifts, dramatic falling‑outs, and at least three separate declarations that their friendship is “officially over.”

And yet, every time a real threat looms or a truly great meal is on the line, the Matzo Mercenary shows up—ready to fight at Dr. Chopsticks’ side, and then immediately argue about who really won afterward.

The Mastermind
Distant Puppeteer • Arcane Investor • Architect Of Chaos

The Mastermind is the unseen hand behind half the chaos in Dr. Chopsticks’ world. He rarely appears in person; instead, he plays the others like puppets from afar, nudging rivalries, friendships, and late‑night battles into place with careful, calculated malice. To Dr. Chopsticks, The Lanky Leaf, and the Matzo Mercenary, he feels less like a person and more like a curse the universe forgot to patch out.

From his remote stronghold, he has quietly amassed a small fortune through questionable ventures and shady business fronts, many of which are whispered about only in half‑deleted forum threads and broken links. Some say his earliest experiments in the arcane were funded by obscure online storefronts and strange domains like a forgotten “tears and trinkets” shop , long since buried in the darker corners of the web.

With that wealth, the Mastermind has pushed beyond conventional crime into occult experimentation—blending technology, sorcery, and psychological manipulation. He doesn’t just want power; he wants to see what happens when you give flawed heroes impossible choices and watch them crack. For now, he watches the trio from a distance, pulling strings, setting traps, and waiting for the moment when every alliance he’s twisted finally snaps.
